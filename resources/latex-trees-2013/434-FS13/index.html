LIN 434 Computer Tree Drawing Workshop (FS13)

 Instructions

  1. If possible, bring a laptop/netbook. Otherwise, you can share with a neighbor.
  2. Before the workshop:
    1. Follow the instructions on the previous page to download and install a text editor on your computer.
    2. Play around with the text editor (try selecting several lines of text and pressing tab, then shift-tab) and the PHP Tree Drawer to get a feel for how they work.
  3. Download the tutorial file and open it in your text editor.
    1. Right click and choose "Open With" or the equivalent on your computer, or open the editor first and open the file from there (File -> Open).
  4. Open another editor window to do your work (File -> New Window).
  5. Open the PHP Tree Drawer and LaTeX Web Previewer in new tabs in your browser.
  6. Follow the instructions in the tutorial.
    1. As you work through the tutorial, keep all of your bracketed trees in the text file in your editor, and copy them into the web previewers when you want to draw them.
